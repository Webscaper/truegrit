Mission control

True grit, is a community corporation for players that enjoy solo, but also small gang pvp. We pride ourselves on being civil and helpful toward our fellow capsuleers, humble in victory and defeat alike.

Here is what we hope to accomplish.

  • Blow things up in PvP
  • Teach new and old players to PvP
  • Contribute positively to the EvE community
  • Create a community where solo PvP'ers of all skill levels can share fits, tactics, killmails and experiences
  • Provide an up-to-date resource for educational PvP content

Space Police

In true grit there are very few rules. Our corporation is not about influencing the way you play, but rather providing a community and resource you can use when playing. We do however have a few rules to ensure we contribute in a positive way to the community of EvE Online.

  • Be civil and respectful
  • Do not break the CCP rules, everything else goes
